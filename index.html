About The Robotics Club

The Robotics Club of SRM University, Vadapalani was founded in 2010 by the departments of Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. Over the past 4 years, the club has grown from its humble beginnings to a full-fledged club by hosting various renowned guest lecturers and by organizing numerous workshops.
